Sponsorship Prospectus

We're very proud to be organizing the third Cascadia Ruby conference. In 2011 and 2012, 150 Rubyists from all across North America and Europe came to Seattle to join us. In addition to our attendees, viewers world-wide participated via our justin.tv live stream.

They heard from from a variety of top Rubyists, enjoyed great networking opportunities, and celebrated our region's rich Ruby history. Videos are available from Confreaks.

Sponsoring Cascadia Ruby is a great way to reach the most interested and engaged Rubyists from the region and beyond. You'll be recognized on our website, in our videos, and at the conference. Your sponsorship will put your business in front of our attendees and viewers. You'll also have the opportunity to attend Cascadia Ruby. And, of course, you'll have our eternal gratitude.

Sponsorship Packages

We've put together three sponsorship packages, detailed below. If none of them feel quite right, we'd be delighted to work with you to design a custom sponsorship package. There are many opportunities to sponsor specific aspects of the conference, such as meals, power, coffee, and drinks. You'll have additional exposure and the adoration of our attendees. If you'd like to explore these options, please get in touch.

In addition to the levels described below, we also provide a Supporter Ticket ($500) for individuals and a Startup Supporter ($1000) ticket for small businesses who would like to contribute. Just select that option at registration and you'll be recognized on the site and thanked during the conference.

Get In Touch
